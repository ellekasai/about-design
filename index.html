大学を卒業して初めての仕事は営業でした

I was born in Phillippines and raised in Kanagawa, Japan. After graduating from Gakushuin Women's College with a Marketing degree in 2012, I started working at a payment startup in Tokyo as a sales associate.

But I've always loved to express myself and be creative, and I wasn't quite satisfied with my job. So after a year, I decided to pursue a completely different career in UI design.

私の大学での専攻はマーケティング｡力をつけるのに時間がかかるアートやデザインのベースがなかったので､まずはHTMLとCSSのコーディングから勉強を開始することにしました｡

シリコンバレーで修業して日本で就職した

I left my job, stayed with my boyfriend in Silicon Valley, and learned coding and design from him.

Eventually I came back to Japan, and in Fall 2013, I became a junior designer at BizReach, a tech startup in Tokyo. I've worked on various projects, including coding the entire frontend of BizReach Woman.

コーディングやデザインの勉強を重ねるにつれ､私はカンファレンスへの参加にも興味をもつようになりました｡スピーカーから各々の分野について深い知識やノウハウを得られるだけでなく､世界各国からやってきた参加者とデザインやコーディングについて話すことができるカンファレンス｡とても刺激を受けられるので､定期的に参加しています｡

学んだことを色んな場面で話してる

TALK 1 / HTML & CSS SKETCHING, ON YOUTUBE

January - February 2014 / Online, in Japanese

I recorded tutorial screencasts of HTML & CSS Sketching on YouTube. The content director of Schoo (a Udemy for Japan) stumbled upon my videos and offered me a speaking opportunity, which led to my talk #2.

TALK 2 / INTRODUCTION TO HTML & CSS SKETCHING

March - April 2014 / Online, in Japanese

Schoo is Japan’s leading online learning platform. I designed and taught a bi-weekly class on HTML/CSS. On every lecture, I did live coding and there were ~500 live viewers.

TALK 3 / WOMEN WHO CODE TOKYO #1

July 2014 / Tokyo, in Japanese

I was an organizer and the keynote speaker at the inaugural Women Who Code event in Tokyo. I spoke about how I learned to code, in front of ~20 female programmers.

TALK 4 / PODCAST

July 2014 / Tokyo, in Japanese

2015年に入ってからPodcastの配信にもチャレンジしています｡Podcastではデザインやコーディングに関する日々の勉強内容について話しています｡現在320人以上のSubscribeがいます｡

2013年に初めてCSSのカンファレンスに参加して以来､自分自身も教える側になりたいと思うようになりました｡ そこで､During my free time, I've taught an online HTML/CSS course and spoke at coding-related events. 今のところまだ日本語でしか話したことがないですが､ I hope to one day speak at an international design conference such as CSSConf.

今オープンソースこんなことやってる

purplecoat.js

Purplecoat.js lets you create labeled overlays that can be triggered with a click. And you don't need to write a single line of Javascript.

ResumeCards

ResumeCards is a Markdown based resume generator. It looks great on mobile/desktop and can be saved as PDF.

Twemoji Awesome

Like Font Awesome, but for Twitter Emoji. Works with Emoji Cheat Sheet.

Shiori

Shiori is a Bootstrap-based Jekyll theme. It looks great by default, but you can customize it easily. It supports Bootstrap and Font Awesome classes out of the box.
